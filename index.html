Play Rock Paper Scissors

Rules

  • In this version of Rock Paper Scissors, you'll play against the Computer.
  • Each winning round is worth 1 point. The player with 5 points wins the game.
  • Before each round, you must choose which “hand” signal you wish to play.
  • The outcome of a round is determined by 3 simple rules:
    • Rock wins against scissors.
    • Scissors win against paper.
    • Paper wins against rock.
